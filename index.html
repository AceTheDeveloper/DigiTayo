Our Mission: Empowering Youth with Digital Literacy

DigiTayo strives to provide Filipino youth with the knowledge and skills to thrive in the digital world. Our mission is to foster safe, informed, and responsible online behavior, while promoting digital skills that prepare them for future opportunities.

  • Deliver digital literacy education to underserved communities.
  • Promote safe online practices and protect against digital threats.
  • Equip the youth with future-ready skills for career success.

Our Vision: A Digitally Empowered Generation

We envision a future where every Filipino youth has the tools and knowledge to make informed decisions in the digital age. Our vision is a society where technology serves as a bridge to opportunities, personal growth, and social empowerment.

  • Build a community of tech-savvy, responsible digital citizens.
  • Foster digital innovation and creativity for social good.
  • Enable access to quality digital resources for all youth.

Got Questions? Check out our FAQs

Find answers to your questions about our upcoming events and campaigns. We're here to help you make the most of your experience!

What types of events do you offer?

We offer workshops, campaigns, and webinars focusing on digital literacy, cybersecurity, and online education. Join us to learn valuable skills for your personal and professional growth.

How can I register for an event?

Simply visit the event page and click the "Register Now" button. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details you need to participate.

Are your events free?

Yes! All of our events are completely free to attend. We're committed to making digital literacy accessible to everyone.

Can I participate if I'm new to digital tools?

Absolutely! Our workshops and events cater to all skill levels, from beginners to experts. You'll learn at your own pace with guidance from our team.

What do I need to join an event?

For most events, you just need a device with internet access. We recommend checking the event details for any specific requirements, such as software or tools to be used.

Can I ask questions during the event?

Yes! All our events allow for interactive Q&A sessions. You'll have the chance to ask questions directly to the instructors or event hosts.
